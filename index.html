Miniswap is a decentralized liquidity protocol based on Ethereum.
No market maker is required, the liquidity provider only needs to deposit funds into the liquidity pool, and through simple formal equations, it can provide liquidity for thousands of users.
Trading is staking
  • Trader
    100% refund of transaction fees
  • Liquidity provider
    Get 100% fee income from the platform
  • oken holders
    Get 50% fee dividend from the platform
  • Miniswap
    Daily burning value 50% token of the platform fee
How to get Miniswap token MINI

The total amount of MINI is 1 billion, of which 15% will be issued in the form of private placement. Before the product goes online, the ETH/MINI private equity pool will be opened first. The user redeems a certain amount of ETH into the fund pool to obtain the redeemed MINI.

The fund pool uses a ladder exchange algorithm: In the first round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 100901MINI, with a total of 2018039 pieces.

In the second round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 93838MINI, with a total of 2815164 pieces. In the tenth round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 52510MINI, with a total of 6776154 pieces. And so on...
