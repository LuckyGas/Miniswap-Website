Miniswap is a decentralized liquidity protocol based on Ethereum.
No market maker is required, the liquidity provider only needs to deposit funds into the liquidity pool, and through simple formal equations, it can provide liquidity for thousands of users.
Trading is staking
  • Trader
    100% refund of transaction fees
  • Liquidity provider
    Get 100% fee income from the platform
  • oken holders
    Get 50% fee dividend from the platform
  • Miniswap
    Daily burning value 50% token of the platform fee
How to get Miniswap token X

The total supply of token X is 21,000,000, of which 40% will be issued in the form of private placement. Before the product launch, the ETH/X private fund pool will be opened first. Users exchange a certain amount of ETH into the fund pool to obtain the redeemed X, and the transaction fee will be 100% refunded in X.

The fund pool uses a ladder exchange algorithm:

When initially launched, 1ETH can be exchanged for 100X, with a total of 10,000 After 10,000 are exchanged, 1ETH can be exchanged for 90X, with a total of 10,000 And so on.
